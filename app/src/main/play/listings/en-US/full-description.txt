Echobell turns webhooks and channel events into reliable Android alerts.

Create channels, subscribe this device, and receive records from services, scripts, automations, monitoring tools, and email triggers. Direct keys make it easy to trigger alerts without managing a full channel.

Key features:

- Channel and direct webhook notifications
- Time-sensitive and call-style alert modes for urgent events
- Custom title, body, link, condition, and note templates
- Notification history stored on this device
- Channel sharing and subscription controls
- Premium support for unlimited channels, direct keys, and alert modes
- Privacy, terms, and account deletion controls in settings

Echobell is built for developers, operators, and anyone who needs actionable alerts from their own systems.
